About

Hi! I'm Nell. I am a third-year physics graduate student at UC Berkeley studying particle theory, with interests in QCD and thermal field theory. My advisor is Hitoshi Murayama.

I did my undergraduate at MIT and worked with Jesse Thaler in jet physics and collider phenomenology. In addition to my research, I care a lot about making physics a better place for women, people of color, and queer people. While at MIT I had the opportunity to work with the physics department administration on their new Physics Community Values.

Outside of physics I spend most of my time talking and thinking about gender and trans-related stuff, doing pretend science in RPGs, and sometimes making music.
